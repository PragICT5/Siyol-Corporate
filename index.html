Siyol International

Siyol International is a diversified and forward-thinking group of companies, established as a leader in providing high-quality international trade and engineering services. With a presence in Singapore and Sri Lanka, we specialize in delivering innovative solutions that bridge industries, markets, and technologies. Our expertise spans a wide range of sectors, ensuring that we meet the evolving needs of our clients with precision and excellence. Whether facilitating global trade or providing cutting-edge engineering services, Siyol International is committed to fostering sustainable growth and building lasting partnerships worldwide.

With a commitment to excellence, we are devoted to driving growth and innovation by delivering world-class products and services that generate lasting value for our partners and clients around the globe.

KEY VERTICALS

Siyol's telecommunications division provides installation, maintenance, and support services. Our offerings include CPE, 4G LTE & 5G devices, Wi-Fi extenders, mesh systems, access points, Android & AOSP set-top boxes, IP/PSTN phones, telecom equipment, servers, and data center solutions.

We are a leading provider to the healthcare sector, with strategic partnerships with global brands in oncology, theater, hospital equipment, and instrumentation.

We provide IT services including software development, server deployment, cloud provisioning, network setup, and wireless infrastructure support.

We specialize in delivering bespoke engineering projects across telecommunications, healthcare, and other sectors.

Optical Networking Units
